Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Chrome extension "Shopsense AI".

Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you when you use the Extension:
  • Amazon product reviews: We collect and analyze product reviews from Amazon to provide you with relevant information and assist you in making informed decisions about products.
  • Chat conversations: We collect and process the chat conversations between you and our chatbot for the purpose of improving the functionality and performance of the chatbot.
  • User name: If you provide us with your name or if your name is associated with your user account, we may collect your name. We use your name to personalize your interactions with the chatbot and improve your overall user experience.
  • City-level location: We may collect your city-level location from your IP address. We use this information to improve the relevance of the product recommendations we provide to you.

Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect from you to:
  • Improve the chatbot: We use the information collected from your chat conversations to train and enhance the performance of our chatbot, making it more accurate and efficient in providing relevant product information.
  • Personalize your experience: We use your name to personalize your interactions with the chatbot and improve your overall user experience.

Information Sharing

We do not share any of the information collected through the Extension with any third parties, except as necessary to provide the services you have requested. For example, we may share your chat conversations with our developers to help them improve the chatbot.

Data Retention

We retain the information collected through the Extension for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. However, we will delete your personal information upon request or when it is no longer needed.

Information Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Minors' Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, we will take steps to delete such information.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at team@shopsense.xyz.

Do Not Track Features

Our Extension does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals. We may track users' activities on our Extension for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at team@shopsense.xyz.

Review, Update, or Delete Your Data

If you would like to review, update, or delete the personal information we have collected from you, please contact us at team@shopsense.xyz.
